Frédérique Duyrat is an ancient historian and numismatist. She is the Director of Collections and the Keeper of the Heberden Coin Room at the Ashmolean Museum, Oxford. Together with Ulrike Peter she is responsible for the Greek numismatics working group, and with Andrew Meadows and Pere Pau Ripollès for the [ARCH project](https://greekcoinage.org/arch/).
