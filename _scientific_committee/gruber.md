Ethan is the Metadata Librarian at the University of Virginia Library, working on a variety of cultural heritage projects across the Library, Archive, and Museum domains at the university and beyond. He is responsible for Linked Open Data modeling and software architecture at Nomisma.org, and [Kerameikos.org](http://kerameikos.org).
